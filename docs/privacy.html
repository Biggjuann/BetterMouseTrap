Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 13, 2025

MouseTrap - Idea Engine ("MouseTrap," "we," "us," or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

Information We Collect

Account Information: When you create an account, we collect your email address and an encrypted password. We use this solely for authentication purposes.

User-Generated Content: Product names and URLs you enter for idea generation, and the ideas, specs, and patent searches produced during your sessions. This content is stored to provide your idea history feature.

Usage Data: We may collect anonymous usage statistics such as feature usage frequency to improve the app experience. We do not collect device identifiers or location data.

How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and maintain the MouseTrap service
  • To authenticate your account
  • To store your idea history so you can revisit past sessions
  • To generate AI-powered product ideas and patent searches based on your inputs
  • To improve and optimize the app experience

Third-Party Services

MouseTrap uses the following third-party services:

  • AI Language Model APIs: Your product inputs are sent to AI services to generate ideas and concept specs. These inputs are not used to train AI models.
  • USPTO PatentsView API: Patent search queries are sent to the public USPTO PatentsView database to find prior art.

Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored on secure servers. Passwords are encrypted and never stored in plain text. We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information.

Data Retention

Your account data and idea history are retained as long as your account is active. You may request deletion of your account and associated data at any time by contacting us.

Children's Privacy

MouseTrap is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this page.

Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or wish to request data deletion, please contact us at:
