Frequently Asked Questions

How does MouseTrap generate ideas?
MouseTrap uses AI to analyze any product you enter and generates a portfolio of sellable business ideas across five strategic tiers: Top 3, Moonshot, Upgrades, Adjacent, and Recurring revenue models.

How does the patent search work?
MouseTrap searches the USPTO PatentsView database for existing patents similar to your idea. It scores each result for relevance so you can assess freedom to operate before investing time or money.

Can I export my ideas?
Yes. Tap any idea to view its full concept spec, then use the PDF export button to generate a polished one-page document perfect for pitching or patent filings.

Is my data private?
Yes. Your product inputs and generated ideas are stored securely and are only accessible to you. See our Privacy Policy for details.
